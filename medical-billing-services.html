Medical & Mental Health Care Billing Services

Expert MedBills provides comprehensive billing services for medical and mental health care providers, from enrollment to payment posting and patient billing. Our team of experts ensures that claims are accurately coded, submitted and followed up on with insurance companies to ensure timely and accurate reimbursement.
We provide customized solutions tailored to the unique needs of your practice to minimize administrative burdens and maximize your revenue. With cutting-edge technology and streamlined processes, you can trust us to handle your billing needs and focus on patient care.

“Medical billing service is a process of submitting and following up on claims with health insurance companies. Medical billing services help healthcare providers streamline their billing processes, minimize denials and rejections, and maximize revenue by ensuring timely and accurate payment for services.”
What is Medical Billing?

We offer a wide range of comprehensive medical and mental health care billing services designed to streamline your revenue cycle and ensure accurate and timely reimbursement for your services. Our team of experts has extensive experience in the healthcare industry, which allows us to offer personalized solutions that cater to the specific needs of your practice. Here are some of the key billing services that we provide:

Enrollment Services: We handle the entire enrollment process with insurance companies, ensuring that your practice is properly credentialed and ready to submit claims.

Claims Submission: We submit claims electronically and follow up with insurance companies to ensure timely reimbursement.

Payment Posting: We accurately post payments and adjustments to patient accounts, ensuring that your revenue cycle runs smoothly.

Denial Management: We identify the causes of denied claims and take appropriate action to appeal or resubmit them.

Patient Billing: We handle all patient billing and collections, providing accurate and timely statements to ensure timely payment.

Monthly Reports: We provide detailed monthly reports that give you an overview of your practice's financial performance.
